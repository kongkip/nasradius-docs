## Overview

Payment Settings allows you to configure mobile money integration for receiving payments.

<Note>
  This tab is only available in **Hotspot mode**.
</Note>

## Supported Providers

| Provider | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| M-PESA | Direct Safaricom integration |
| Kopokopo | Payment aggregator |

## M-PESA Configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select M-PESA">
    Choose M-PESA from the provider dropdown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Mode">
    Select Till or Paybill mode
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Fill in Consumer Key, Consumer Secret, Shortcode, Till Number, and Passkey
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create** or **Update**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Kopokopo Configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Kopokopo">
    Choose Kopokopo from the provider dropdown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Fill in Client ID, Client Secret, and STK Till Number
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create** or **Update**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Testing

After configuration:
1. Make a small test payment
2. Verify it appears in Payments
3. Check customer balance is credited

<Warning>
  Use production credentials for live payments, not sandbox.
</Warning>
