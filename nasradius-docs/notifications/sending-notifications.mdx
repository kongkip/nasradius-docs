## Overview

You can manually send SMS notifications to customers for:
- Custom announcements
- Promotional messages
- Individual customer communication

## Sending a Notification

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Send Notification">
    Click the **Send Notification** button at the top of the Notifications page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Customer">
    Choose the recipient customer from the dropdown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose Message">
    Type your message in the text field
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send">
    Click **Send** to dispatch the notification
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Message Guidelines

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="text-width" title="Character Limits">
    Standard SMS: 160 characters. Longer messages split into multiple SMS.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="language" title="Language">
    Use clear, simple language that customers understand
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Branding">
    Include your ISP name for recognition
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Message Templates

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Payment Confirmation">
    ```
    Hi [Customer], your payment of KSh [Amount] has been received. 
    Balance: KSh [Balance]. Thank you for choosing [ISP Name]!
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Plan Activation">
    ```
    Your [Plan Name] is now active! Valid until [Expiry Date]. 
    Enjoy your internet! - [ISP Name]
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Low Balance">
    ```
    Hi [Customer], your balance is low (KSh [Balance]). 
    Top up now to stay connected! - [ISP Name]
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| SMS not sending | Check SMS provider configuration |
| Wrong number | Verify customer phone number |
| Delivery failed | Check SMS provider balance |

<Warning>
  Ensure you have configured your SMS provider in Settings before sending notifications.
</Warning>
