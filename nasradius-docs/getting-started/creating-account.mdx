## Overview

NasRadius uses a multi-tenant architecture where each ISP gets their own dedicated subdomain. This ensures data isolation and personalized branding for your business.

## Requesting Your Account

To get started with NasRadius, you'll need to request a tenant account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact Sales">
    Reach out to our sales team via:
    - **Email**: sales@nasradius.com
    - **WhatsApp**: +254 724 440 202
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Business Details">
    Share the following information:
    - Your ISP/Business name
    - Preferred subdomain (e.g., `yourisp.nasradius.com`)
    - Contact phone number
    - Email address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Credentials">
    Once approved, you'll receive:
    - Your tenant subdomain URL
    - Admin username
    - Temporary password
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First-Time Login

<Warning>
  For security, you should change your password after your first login.
</Warning>

1. Navigate to your tenant URL (e.g., `https://yourisp.nasradius.com`)
2. Enter your provided username
3. Enter your temporary password
4. Click **Sign In**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/login-page.png" alt="NasRadius Login Page" />
</Frame>

## What You'll See After Login

After successfully logging in, you'll be redirected to the **Dashboard**. The dashboard provides an overview of your business metrics including:

- Daily and weekly revenue
- Customer statistics
- Online customers
- Revenue trends

<Note>
  By default, the system opens in **PPPoE mode**. You can switch to **Hotspot mode** using the toggle buttons at the top of each page.
</Note>

## Account Security Best Practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Strong Password" icon="lock">
    Use a password with at least 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  </Card>
  <Card title="Secure Connection" icon="shield-check">
    Always access NasRadius over HTTPS (the default)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Session Management" icon="clock">
    Log out when not using the system, especially on shared computers
  </Card>
  <Card title="Browser Security" icon="browser">
    Keep your browser updated and avoid saving passwords on public computers
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
