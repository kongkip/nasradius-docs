## Overview

The Analytics tab provides insights into your Kopa system's performance.

## Stat Cards

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Kopa Customers" icon="users">
    Number of customers using Kopa
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Kopa Revenue" icon="coins">
    Total revenue generated through Kopa
  </Card>
  <Card title="Plans Sold" icon="ticket">
    Number of plans purchased via Kopa
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outstanding Balance" icon="wallet">
    Total amount customers owe
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Performance Insights

The analytics section shows:
- Customer activity trends
- Revenue patterns
- Daily metrics summary
- Last update timestamp

## Daily Metrics

Recent activity summary includes:
- Date of activity
- Revenue for that day
- Plans sold count

## Key Indicators

| Metric | Good Sign | Warning Sign |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| Customers | Growing | Declining |
| Revenue | Increasing | Stagnant |
| Outstanding | Low ratio | High ratio |
| Plans Sold | Consistent | Dropping |
