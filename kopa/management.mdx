## Overview

The Management tab allows you to:
- View customers with Kopa balances
- Track outstanding amounts
- Manage Kopa accounts

## Customer Management

View all customers who have used Kopa with their:
- Account information
- Outstanding balance
- Payment history
- Credit status

## Outstanding Balances

Monitor and manage amounts owed:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Current | Within payment period |
| Overdue | Past payment deadline |
| Paid | Fully settled |

## Best Practices

- Follow up on overdue balances regularly
- Set reasonable Kopa limits
- Track payment patterns
- Communicate with customers about outstanding amounts
