## Overview

The Hotspot Dashboard provides a complete view of your WiFi hotspot business, including real-time customer activity, revenue analytics, and performance metrics.

## Stat Cards

The top section displays six interactive stat cards with sparkline trends:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Daily Customers" icon="user-plus">
    New customers registered today with 30-day trend
  </Card>
  <Card title="Daily Revenue" icon="coins">
    Total revenue collected today with 30-day trend
  </Card>
  <Card title="Weekly Revenue" icon="calendar">
    This week's revenue with 4-week trend
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Customers" icon="users">
    All registered customers with cumulative growth
  </Card>
  <Card title="Total Revenue" icon="sack-dollar">
    All-time revenue with cumulative trend
  </Card>
  <Card title="Online Customers" icon="signal">
    Currently connected users with 24-hour activity
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Charts and Analytics

### Monthly Revenue & Transactions

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/monthly-revenue-chart.png" alt="Monthly Revenue Chart" />
</Frame>

This dual-axis bar chart shows:
- **Monthly Revenue** (left axis) - Total payments received
- **Transactions** (right axis) - Number of payment transactions
- **Monthly Bill (4%)** - Your NasRadius service fee

### Weekly Revenue

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/weekly-revenue-chart.png" alt="Weekly Revenue" />
</Frame>
Toggle between current and previous week to analyze day-by-day performance:

| Day | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| Monday - Sunday | Daily revenue breakdown |
| Toggle buttons | Switch between "This Week" and "Last Week" |

<Tip>
  Use this chart to identify your busiest days and plan promotions accordingly.
</Tip>

### Plan Performance Analysis

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/plan-performance.png" alt="Plan Performance" />
</Frame>

This section allows you to:

1. **View all plans**: See purchase frequency for each internet plan
2. **Select a specific plan**: Get detailed metrics including:
   - Number of purchases
   - Daily purchase frequency
   - Weekly purchase frequency
   - Monthly purchase frequency

### Top 10 Customers

A horizontal bar chart showing your highest-spending customers. Use this to:
- Identify VIP customers
- Target retention campaigns
- Understand customer value

### Revenue by NAS

If you have multiple Network Access Servers (routers), this chart breaks down revenue by location:

```
Each bar represents monthly revenue for a specific NAS device
Colors differentiate between different NAS locations
```

<Info>
  This is particularly useful for ISPs with multiple hotspot locations to understand which sites perform best.
</Info>

### Daily SMS Uses

Track your SMS notification usage over the past 30 days:
- Monitor SMS costs
- Identify peak notification periods
- Plan SMS budget accordingly

## Understanding the Data

### Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Calculation |
|--------|-------------|
| Daily Revenue | Sum of payments received since midnight |
| Weekly Revenue | Sum of payments from Monday 00:00 |
| Total Revenue | All-time sum of payments |
| Monthly Bill | 4% of monthly revenue (NasRadius fee) |

### Customer Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Daily Customers | Accounts created today |
| Total Customers | All registered accounts |
| Online Customers | Active sessions right now |

## Sparkline Trends

Each stat card includes a mini trend chart:
- **Green line**: Positive trend (upward)
- **Neutral line**: Stable performance
- Hover to see specific data points
