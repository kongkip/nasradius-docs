## Overview

Your ISP name appears throughout the NasRadius interface, including:
- The browser tab title
- The sidebar header
- Customer-facing notifications (SMS)

Customizing this ensures a professional, branded experience.

## Accessing General Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Click on **Settings** in the left sidebar navigation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open General Settings Tab">
    The **General Settings** tab should be selected by default. If not, click on it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Changing Your ISP Name

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Click the **Edit** icon (pencil) next to your current settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update ISP Name">
    Enter your desired ISP name in the **ISP Name** field
    
    <Tip>
      Use your official business name for consistency across all communications
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Contact Phone (Optional)">
    Enter your customer support phone number in the **Contact Phone Number** field
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the **Save Settings** button to apply your changes
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="spell-check" title="Keep It Short">
    Use a concise name that fits well in the interface (ideally under 20 characters)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Use Official Name">
    Match your registered business name for professionalism
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="phone" title="Valid Phone Number">
    Ensure the contact number is reachable for customer support
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<Warning>
  If changes don't appear immediately, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.
</Warning>

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Name not saving | Ensure all required fields are filled |
| Name not updating in tab | Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5) |
| Old name showing | Clear browser cache and cookies |
