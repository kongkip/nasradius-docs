## Welcome to NasRadius

NasRadius is a comprehensive ISP billing and management platform designed specifically for Internet Service Providers. Whether you're managing a Hotspot network or PPPoE connections, NasRadius provides all the tools you need to run your business efficiently.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Customer Management">
    Easily track and manage customer data, balances, account information, and connection history all in one place.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Real-time Analytics">
    Monitor your business with comprehensive dashboards showing revenue, customer trends, and performance metrics.

## Quick Start

Get started with NasRadius in just a few steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Request your tenant subdomain and receive your login credentials
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set up your ISP name, SMS provider, and payment integrations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Internet Plans">
    Define your service packages with speeds, data limits, and pricing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Customers">
    Start onboarding customers and managing their subscriptions
  </Step>
</Steps>
